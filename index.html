Our mission is to help every family capture, protect, and meaningfully use its lived wisdom.

Through Morb (the living family archive), MIA (a respectful AI companion), and Harmony Well (personalised wellness), we connect past and present so that memories don’t just sit in boxes—they show up when you need them: to spark conversations, guide decisions, and support mental health.

Please take the surveys below and help us shape the future of family legacy.

Morb — The Living Family Archive

Morb brings photos, videos, family-tree details, and even DNA insights into one secure, searchable home. It auto-tags faces and moments, lets you send time-capsule messages to the future, and makes it easy to share memories privately with the people who matter. You stay in control—what’s saved, what’s shared, and what’s deleted is always your choice.

MIA — Your AI Memory Companion

MIA (Memory Intelligent Assistant) helps families talk—and remember. With your permission, it nudges thoughtful questions (“Ask Dad about his first job”), transcribes spoken stories, and turns casual chats into keepsakes you can find later. MIA stays respectful and optional: you decide when it listens and what gets saved.

Take the MIA Survey

Harmony Well — Wellness Rooted in Heritage

Harmony Well blends evidence-based tools—CBT chat, guided meditations, sleep stories—with the strength of your family narrative. It surfaces supportive reflections (“How your grandmother handled exam stress”), helping you build routines that feel personal and grounded. Insights are private, optional to share, and designed to support—not replace—professional care.
